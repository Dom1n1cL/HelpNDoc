How to add a cabinet to your quote

To add a cabinet click on the button titled 'cabinet' then select a cabinet group, a cabinet type then a cabinet. After you have selected your cabinet type in your quantity, height, width and depth. Then select your hinges you have the option of left or right. You also have the option to add doors onto your cabinet if you wish to select 'yes' , the doors will be calculated with a 2mm clearence at the top and bottem, though please note non-standard door/panel sizes must be ordered seperately. Once you are happy click on the button 'save' .

If you wish to know the cabinet specifications for your specific cabinet click on the button on the top right hand corner titled 'cabinet specifications' , here you can see the sizing details, drawer details, shelf details and hardware description.
